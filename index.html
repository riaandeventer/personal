Who is Riaan Deventer?

Bio

Riaan was born and grew up in the Western Cape of South Africa, where he also completed his matric in 1990. During that time he excelled in sport as a healthy way of life and he still maintains this lifestyle today. He completed his Bachelor of Commerce degree in economics and management sciences at the University of Stellenbosch. Because of his love for people he then also later completed two bachelor degrees in ministry. Riaan is the Business Manager for Alethes Legacy Consulting, a consulting company specializing in business management and administration, property investing, financing and risk management as well as people skills and leadership development.

Riaan's first major introduction to the world of work was in 1998, when he worked as a Database Administrator & Systems Support Programmer with BoE Bank Business Division (Now Nedbank) in Paarl, Western Cape (South Africa's largest bank at the time). In 2002, after a solid foundation in the banking industry, he worked in sales. In 2005 Riaan pursued his passion for property and excelled in property investing, sales, and rentals; covering all facets of these processes. It was in June of 2008 that he started with Alethes Legacy Consulting by applying his knowledge and experience in sales and business development to help build companies that can impact future generations.

Through Alethes Legacy Consulting, Riaan has trained and facilitated workshops for corporate staff in Presentation Skills, Time Management, Conflict Management, Emotional Intelligence, Business Writing, etc. Riaan has filled the position of Africa Operations Manager for Quiari LLC, an international health management company requiring the management of operations in 7 African Countries. Riaan is also a certified coach, mentor, and facilitator for I CAN John Maxwell Leadership Development, Coaching and Mentorship Program and occupied the position of Business Manager for I CAN Leadership Institute AFRICA.

Riaan is a natural leader, loves teamwork, and is passionate about people development. He believes in the consistent growth of self and those around him. Riaan's mission in life is to continually grow, wisely allocate, equitably share and responsibly manage the resources available to him in the process of developing character and competence in order to build a life that keeps on giving to present and future generations.
